About FluentBit

Empowering cloud-native logging and observability

Who We Are

FluentBit is an open-source, high-performance logging and metrics processor designed for modern cloud-native environments. Born from the need for a lightweight yet powerful solution, FluentBit has become the go-to choice for organizations seeking efficient log aggregation and data pipeline management.

We are a community-driven project backed by industry leaders and supported by thousands of contributors worldwide. Our commitment is to provide a vendor-neutral, flexible, and reliable logging solution that scales from edge devices to massive cloud deployments.

Since 2015

Serving the community

Our Mission

To provide the most efficient, lightweight, and flexible logging solution for cloud-native environments. We strive to empower organizations with tools that make observability accessible, reliable, and performant.

Our mission extends beyond just processing logs—we aim to create an ecosystem where data flows seamlessly across platforms, enabling teams to focus on insights rather than infrastructure.

Our Vision

We envision a world where observability is democratized—where every organization, regardless of size, has access to enterprise-grade logging infrastructure. FluentBit aims to be the universal standard for log processing in cloud-native architectures.

Through continuous innovation and community collaboration, we're building toward a future where data collection and processing are effortless, secure, and intelligent.
